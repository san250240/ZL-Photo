Because someday, this will all be a blur – let’s make it unforgettable

Boston documentary wedding photographer with a soft spot for multicultural, LGBTQ+, and intimate weddings

I’m a Boston-based wedding and elopement photographer who captures love in all its forms. I’m especially drawn to multicultural weddings, LGBTQ+ love stories, and intimate celebrations, but no matter the setting, I care most about telling your story with authenticity. My approach is documentary-style with vibrant photos that make you say “that’s so us!”

Some days deserve to be remembered.

When you work with me, you’ll get more than beautiful images; you’ll get an easy and personalized experience from start to finish. I’ll guide you when you need it, blend into the background when you don’t, and help you feel totally at ease in front of the camera. I’m here to capture the moments that matter most, because time will blur these moments, but we can make them unforgettable.

In case you're wondering...

How would you describe your photography style?

My style is candid, colorful, and story-driven. I focus on capturing real moments—big emotions, quiet glances, and everything in between—without stiff poses or forced smiles. If you’re into photos that actually feel like your day, we’ll get along great.

How far in advance should we book you?

Popular dates fill up fast (especially spring and fall), so I recommend booking 9–12 months out. That said, don’t hesitate to reach out even if your wedding is sooner—I might still have availability!

What’s included in your wedding packages?

All packages include a pre-wedding consultation, high-resolution edited images, an online gallery for sharing, and printing rights. I also offer full-day coverage, micro wedding options, and custom packages for unique celebrations. Take a look at my photography packages.

Will you be the one photographing our wedding?

100%. When you hire me, you get me. I may bring a second shooter depending on your package, but I’ll be there for all the key moments.

When will we get our photos?

You’ll receive a sneak peek within a few days, and your full gallery will be ready in 4–6 weeks. I know you’ll be excited to relive the day, so I don’t keep you waiting long!
