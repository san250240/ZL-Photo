Engagement Photos You’ll Actually Have Fun Doing

Think of this as a date with a third wheel who just happens to have a camera. I’m here to make sure you get photos that feel like you — the real smiles, the sneaky hand squeezes, the moments that happen in between. No awkward prom poses. No “stand there and look at me” for an hour. Just you two, enjoying yourselves while I document it all in a candid, colorful way.

Why Engagement Sessions Matter

An engagement session is more than just “practice” for your wedding day. Sure, it’s a great way to get comfortable in front of the camera before you’re in full wedding attire. But it’s also a way to tell your story in a way the wedding day might not allow.

These are the photos you’ll use for your save-the-dates, hang in your home, or post every year on your anniversary. They’re the photos that will bring you back to this exact season in your lives: the anticipation, the inside jokes, the way you look at each other when you think no one’s watching.

My Approach: Stress-Free & Fun

You don’t have to “know how to pose” for this. That’s my job. Instead of forcing you into stiff positions, I’ll guide you with little prompts, games, and movement so you can just focus on each other.

I’ve had couples show up swearing they’re “awkward in photos,” then leave laughing and saying they forgot the camera was even there. That’s the goal, for this to feel less like a photoshoot and more like time well spent together.

Location Ideas in Boston

Boston is full of gorgeous spots for engagement photos. Here are a few favorites:

  • Esplanade – Relaxed by the water, with paths and bridges that feel peaceful but still close to the city.

  • Boston Public Garden – Iconic and timeless, with flowers, fountains, and the kind of romance you see in old movies.

  • Castle Island / Carson Beach – Breezy coastal views, golden sunsets, and plenty of room to explore.

  • Arnold Arboretum – Lush and green in the warmer months, with amazing fall color when the leaves change.

  • MIT Area – Modern architecture, unique angles, and some of the best skyline views around.

Not sure where to go? I’ll help you choose the perfect spot that matches your personalities.
