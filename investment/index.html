The Process

1. Say hello
Start by getting in touch through my contact form. Whether you’re months into planning or just getting started, I’d love to hear what you’re dreaming up. Share whatever youve got date, location, general vibe and if you have a vision board, playlist, or favorite color palette, send it along!

2. Lets chat
Once I get your message, Ill send over all the details about packages and pricing. From there, we can set up a time to talk video call, coffee, whatever feels easy and see if we click. You dont need to have all the answers yet. We can just talk through whats important to you and what youre hoping for.

3. Make it official
When youre ready, Ill send over a contract and invoice to lock in your date. Ill be available anytime between now and your wedding if questions pop up or you just want to brainstorm something. Closer to the day, Ill send a questionnaire to help plan out your timeline and photo priorities. Well have a final check-in to make sure everythings feeling smooth and stress-free.

After the Wedding

Once the confettis settled and your feet have recovered from all that dancing.

1. Sneak peeks
Ill send a handful of preview images within the week so you can relive the magic (and share it with your people).

2. Full gallery delivery
Your full gallery will land in your inbox within 810 weeks, ready to be downloaded, shared, and loved. Youll be able to order prints, albums, and anything else youd like to hold in your hands or hang on your walls.
