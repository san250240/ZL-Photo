The Process

1. Say hello
Start by getting in touch through my contact form. Whether you’re months into planning or just getting started, I’d love to hear what you’re dreaming up. Share whatever you’ve got — date, location, general vibe — and if you have a vision board, playlist, or favorite color palette, send it along!

2. Let’s chat
Once I get your message, I’ll send over all the details about packages and pricing. From there, we can set up a time to talk — video call, coffee, whatever feels easy — and see if we click. You don’t need to have all the answers yet. We can just talk through what’s important to you and what you’re hoping for.

3. Make it official
When you’re ready, I’ll send over a contract and invoice to lock in your date. I’ll be available anytime between now and your wedding if questions pop up or you just want to brainstorm something. Closer to the day, I’ll send a questionnaire to help plan out your timeline and photo priorities. We’ll have a final check-in to make sure everything’s feeling smooth and stress-free.

After the Wedding

Once the confetti’s settled and your feet have recovered from all that dancing.

1. Sneak peeks
I’ll send a handful of preview images within the week so you can relive the magic (and share it with your people).

2. Full gallery delivery
Your full gallery will land in your inbox within 8–10 weeks, ready to be downloaded, shared, and loved. You’ll be able to order prints, albums, and anything else you’d like to hold in your hands or hang on your walls.
