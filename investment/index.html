The Process

1. Say hello
Start by getting in touch through my contact form. Whether you’re months into planning or just getting started, I’d love to hear what you’re dreaming up. Share whatever you’ve got — date, location, general vibe — and if you have a vision board, playlist, or favorite color palette, send it along!

2. Let’s chat
Once I get your message, I’ll send over all the details about packages and pricing. From there, we can set up a time to talk — video call, coffee, whatever feels easy — and see if we click. You don’t need to have all the answers yet. We can just talk through what’s important to you and what you’re hoping for.

3. Make it official
When you’re ready, I’ll send over a contract and invoice to lock in your date. I’ll be available anytime between now and your wedding if questions pop up or you just want to brainstorm something. Closer to the day, I’ll send a questionnaire to help plan out your timeline and photo priorities. We’ll have a final check-in to make sure everything’s feeling smooth and stress-free.

After the Wedding

Once the confetti’s settled and your feet have recovered from all that dancing.

1. Sneak peeks
I’ll send a handful of preview images within the week so you can relive the magic (and share it with your people).

2. Full gallery delivery
Your full gallery will land in your inbox within 8–10 weeks, ready to be downloaded and shared. You’ll be able to order prints, albums, and anything else you’d like to hold in your hands or hang on your walls.

Frequently Asked Questions

What’s your photography approach?

I blend candid, documentary-style coverage with gentle guidance when it’s needed. Most of the time, I’m observing quietly, catching the real laughs, subtle glances, and all the in-between moments that make your day yours. I’ll step in when you need direction, but my goal is always to help you feel like yourselves in front of the camera.

How would you describe your editing style?

True-to-life, with a little extra warmth and soul. I aim for timeless colors that feel rich and natural — nothing overly filtered or trendy. Your gallery should feel like your day looked and felt, with a consistent, polished style that holds up for years to come.

Can we customize a package?

Absolutely. Every wedding is different, and your coverage should reflect that. Whether you need something more intimate, want to add extra hours, or are planning a multi-day celebration, I’m happy to tailor a package that fits your plans.

How far in advance should we book?

For full-day weddings, I typically book 9–15 months in advance. Elopements and shorter coverage often have more flexibility, but the sooner you reach out, the better — especially for popular dates during spring and fall. If your date is coming up soon, still reach out! If I’m available, I’d love to chat.

Do you travel outside of Boston?

Yes! I’m based in Boston but love photographing weddings and elopements all over New England (and beyond). Travel within 50 miles of the city is included in most packages, and I’m always happy to go further. Just let me know your venue, and I’ll send custom travel details.
