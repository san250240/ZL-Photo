Wedding Photography Guides

Wedding photography tips and practical advice to make your day feel easy

Planning a wedding is exciting, emotional, and let’s be honest, sometimes a little overwhelming. Between the venue tours, the dress fittings, and the group texts about which shade of green the bridesmaids are wearing, it’s easy for photography to feel like just another thing on your list. But it doesn’t have to be that way.

This page is your go-to resource for all things wedding photography. Whether you’re wondering when to book your photographer, what to wear for your engagement session, or how to plan a timeline that keeps things relaxed and fun, I’ve got you covered. These guides are here to help you feel confident, prepared, and excited about the photos we’ll create together.

As a Boston wedding photographer, I’ve worked with couples in all kinds of settings, from classic New England church ceremonies to vibrant LGBTQ+ weddings, to intimate elopements in the city. And every couple, every day, has taught me something new. These blog posts bring together that experience in an easy-to-read, no-fluff kind of way.
