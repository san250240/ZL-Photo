East Boston Engagement Session at LoPresti Park – Kate & Aaron

  • Engagement Sessions

Summer is finally here and I’m so excited to have Kate and Aaron’s East Boston engagement photos on the blog. We chose to do the session at LoPresti Park because Aaron used to work nearby. It’s a bit of a hidden gem in Eastie, tucked right along the waterfront, and somehow still feels quiet and peaceful even with the city just across the harbor. The Boston skyline view from the pier and boardwalk was absolutely incredible, easily one of my favorite city views in the area.

From the very first email I got from Kate, I knew we were going to click. She was warm and just genuinely excited to get married. She told me that she and Aaron met during a teaching fellowship in Boston Public Schools. Aaron is now a guidance counselor, which immediately hit home for me; I’m a BPS grad myself and I had a high school guidance counselor who really shaped my life. I have a ton of admiration for the work they do and the way they show up for their students every single day.

Kate and Aaron have been together for over five years, and their story just felt so full of intention and care. They got engaged on a quiet beach in Dartmouth, MA, a spot that’s close to their hearts and not far from their wedding venue, Shining Tides in Mattapoisett. The whole south coast of Massachusetts is meaningful to them, which makes it even more special that they’ll be celebrating there.

Their East Boston engagement session was such a sweet way to kick off the summer. We lucked out with perfect weather, a soft golden hour glow and just enough breeze to keep things comfortable. They were relaxed, happy, and so naturally connected with each other that I barely had to give direction. These are the kinds of sessions I love most: low-key, personal, and in a spot that’s meaningful to the couple.

I’m excited for their wedding day at Shining Tides. I already know it’s going to be filled with love and good people. I totally understand why they chose this venue. It’s right on the water, with views of the bay, lots of open lawn space for lawn games during cocktail hour, and this cozy, tucked-away feeling that makes everything feel more intimate. Fun fact: it’s also a YMCA, so you get that blend of charm and community feel that’s honestly hard to find at most wedding venues.

Check out their Shining Tides wedding photos, I promise you’ll love them!
