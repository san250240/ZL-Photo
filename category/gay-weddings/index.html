Gay Weddings

A collection of LGBTQ+ weddings featuring same-sex and queer couples. As a Boston gay wedding photographer, I know how important it is to feel completely comfortable and seen on your wedding day. You deserve to work with someone who understands your story, respects your identities, and knows how to capture your connection in a way that feels honest and true to you.

Whether you’re planning a city hall elopement, a backyard celebration, or a full weekend wedding, I’m here to help you celebrate every part of it. These are the kinds of love stories that matter. The ones that reflect chosen family, quiet support, loud laughter, and everything in between.

If you’re looking for an inclusive and gay wedding photographer who will celebrate you exactly as you are, this is a great place to start. Scroll through to see some of my recent work, when you’re ready, reach out and let’s talk about documenting your love story.
